Anti-Slip

When it comes to bathtub safety, Bath Magic offers two (2) options to prevent slips and falls !

1. Our Anti-Slip surface

Are you concerned about falling in your reglazed bathtub, and prefer the look and feel of an anti-slip surface thats part of the tub itself? Not interested in a safety mat? Bath Magic can as part of the reglazing process, apply an anti-slip application for bathtub bottoms to prevent accidents.Our system is called Stand-Fast™ and it can be installed during the bathtub refinishing process. Once installed Stand-Fast™ is part of the refinished bathtub and will continue to offer protection for the life of the refinished tub.

  • Done with your Reglazing
  • Use in as little as 4 Hours
  • Prevents Slips & Falls
  • Part of the Tub Itself

2. Sure-step mats

Install a PERMANENT vinyl bathmat in your reglazed tub or shower and enjoy the benefits of a safe bathing surface. No more rubber strips, or ugly rubber mats. A variety of sizes are available to choose from. All stock item orders are shipped directly to your home.

  • AVAILABILITY: In Stock, will ship in 2-3 business days
  • Easy To Install Just Peel & Stick
  • Satisfaction Guaranteed
  • Will Not Mold Or Mildew Ever
  • Same Mat Used In Fine Hotels
  • Cover Damaged Bathtub Floors Too!
  • Easily Cuts To Fit Smaller Bathtubs!
