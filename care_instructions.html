Care Instructions

Before you use your tub for the first time:

First, do not touch the reglazed surface until the designated curing time. Do not open windows, or turn on air fans, as this may cause dirt and debris to fall into the fresh reglazing material, resulting in sharp points forming in the surface. If debris does fall into the tub, do not attempt to remove, as these will wear off over the first months use.Water drip bags have been placed over the water spout, to protect against water dripping onto the freshly reglazed surface. Should the bags begin to fill, the customer must replace the bags, or turn off the water flow. At the conclusion of the drying time, carefully remove any protective paper that was left, and discard. Replace the drain screen, and overflow stopper. Immediately recaulk around the DRAIN LIP, and the TUB RIM. Also, if it was not possible for our technicians to remove the overflow linkage, recaulk around the overflow plate also. Failure to apply and maintain a proper caulk seal may lead to water damage, and cause the reglazed material to peel. DO NOT USE SELF ADHESIVE CAULKING STRIPS.

How to clean your reglazed tub:

Generally avoid cleaning your tub for the first 30 days with anything other than a mild dish soap and a soft sponge. After 30 days, clean your tub as required with a NON-AEROSOL soap scum removing cleaner. We strongly recommend Tilex Bathroom Cleaner or Kaboom. Avoid harsh or abrasive cleaners, aerosol cleaners, acids, bleaches, and scratch pads. Do not use DOW SCRUBBING BUBBLES or SOFT SCRUB as these can dull out the luster on your reglazed surface.

General Tips:

  • DO NOT use the surface for the specified drying time.
  • DO NOT store soap, sponges, cloths, bottles or any objects on the finish. We suggest that you purchase a shower rack instead.
  • DO NOT use abrasive cleansers. No Scrubbing Bubbles, Soft Scrub, or aerosol cleaners.
  • DO NOT use bath mats, pillows, etc. with suctions underneath. They may pull at finish and trap moisture.
  • DO NOT pick surface dust off with your finger. Any surface dust will likely dissipate in 3-4 weeks with normal cleaning and usage. Maintain caulking around the tub.
  • DO NOT store any items in a tub or finished surface that might damage or cut the surface.
  • NEVER use bathtub or other glazed surface to clean brushes or any painting related chemicals or materials. Keep Harsh chemicals away from tub. Don't clean or store sharp or abrasive items on surface.
