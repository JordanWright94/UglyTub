The Process

  1. The caulk and overflow stopper are removed, we lightly sand the area, and apply a chemical etch that only needs to stay on for 10 minutes.
  2. Your appliance is then cleaned, dired, and protective paper is applied around it to protect your bathroom.
  3. Primer is applied to the tub, then we apply a topcoat, and we're done!
